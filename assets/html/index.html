Fashion Waste,
A World Wide Problem

Each year, about 92 million tons of textile in the world goes to waste, responsible for 10% of all greenhouse gas emissions.

The fashion industry is also the second-largest consumer of water worldwide, and produces 20 percent of global waste water.

By 2030, we are expected to discard more than 134 million tons of textile. Let's change these numbers around. Are you with us?
